# Prompt for Claude Code: Revryze x IV Nutrition Pilot Proposal

Build a sales proposal web application for **Revryze x IV Nutrition** — a high-stakes B2B sales proposal targeting IV Nutrition franchisees. The presentation should be premium, data-driven, and styled in IV Nutrition's official brand system. Single-page scrolling proposal with an interactive ROI calculator as the centerpiece.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React 18 + TypeScript, Vite, Wouter routing, TanStack Query
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui (Radix UI primitives)
- **Charts**: Recharts
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Backend**: Node.js + Express 5, TypeScript (tsx for dev, esbuild for prod)
- **Project structure**: `client/` (React), `server/` (Express), `shared/` (Drizzle schemas)

## CORE OFFER STRUCTURE

### Pilot & Pricing Model
- **Standard price**: $30,000 per opening for a **100 member guarantee**
- **Volume price**: $25,000 per opening if franchisee signs/agrees to **≥10 openings over the next 12 months**
- **Pilot pricing**: $25,000 (pilots get the volume rate)
- **Franchisee qualification requirements** to invoke the guarantee:
  - Spend **$20,000 on ads**
  - Generate **250 community-driven leads**
- **Refund mechanic**: dollar-for-dollar pro-rata refund based on memberships sold vs the 100 guarantee
  - 50 memberships sold → 50% refund
  - 75 memberships → 25% refund
  - 100+ → $0 refund (full delivery)

### Corporate framing
- Corporate IV Nutrition incurs **$0 cost** — franchisees pay Revryze fees directly
- All membership revenue and royalty uplift = **pure upside** to corporate
- Messaging pillars: science-backed wellness, community-driven growth, derisked franchisee economics

## FRANCHISEE ROI CALCULATOR (the showpiece — get this right)

This is the centerpiece interactive section. Build it as a clean dashboard with sliders on one side and a live-updating ROI display on the other. Default state must show a strong, compelling result the moment the page loads.

### Inputs (sliders)
1. **Memberships Sold** — range 1 to 100 — **default 100**
2. **Average Membership Value** — range $79 to $400/mo — **default $199**
3. **Average Member Lifetime** — range 1 to 20 months — **default 10 months**
4. **Pricing tier toggle** — $30k Standard / $25k Volume or Pilot — **default $25k**

The qualification requirements ($20k ad spend, 250 community leads) are displayed as a fixed reference box near the calculator — NOT sliders. Just show them as the cost-of-entry to invoke the guarantee.

### Live-Updating Outputs (right side of calculator)
All numbers in monospace, color-coded green for positive / red for negative.

1. **Revryze fee paid** (based on pricing tier toggle)
2. **Refund earned** (if memberships sold < 100) — `(100 - sold) / 100 × fee paid`
3. **Net Revryze cost** (fee − refund)
4. **Ad spend** — fixed at $20,000 (the qualification requirement, displayed as part of the franchisee's total investment)
5. **Total franchisee investment** — Net Revryze cost + $20,000 ad spend
6. **Monthly recurring revenue at open** — `memberships sold × avg membership value`
7. **Total membership revenue over lifetime** — `memberships sold × avg membership value × avg lifetime months`
8. **Net profit** — Total membership revenue − Total franchisee investment
9. **ROI multiple** — Total membership revenue ÷ Total franchisee investment (display as "5.2x" style)
10. **Payback period** — months until membership revenue covers total investment

### Default state math check (so you know it's working)
- 100 memberships × $199/mo × 10 months = **$199,000 lifetime revenue**
- $25,000 Revryze + $20,000 ad spend = **$45,000 total investment**
- Net profit: **$154,000**
- ROI: **~4.4x**
- Payback: ~2.3 months
- Refund: $0 (full delivery)

### Visual elements within the calculator
- Progress bar showing memberships sold vs the 100 guarantee, filling `#009547`
- A small Recharts area chart showing cumulative cash flow over the member lifetime — investment going negative at month 0, then climbing into the green as MRR compounds
- Crisp dividers between inputs and outputs
- Smooth animation on number changes (count-up transitions are fine, keep it tasteful)

## FOUNDING MEMBER CAMPAIGN SECTION

### Framework
- **Length**: 16 weeks pre-open
- **Member target**: 100 founders at open
- **Two offers, both with lifetime-locked rates**

### Offer 1: Founding Vital Starter — $79/mo
- $79 down (= first month, rolls into MTM)
- Standard perks: $79 credit, 25mg NAD+ monthly, Compression/Red Light, 6% off
- **Visit One Welcome Bundle (~$389 value)**:
  - Mega Myers IV ($179)
  - NAD 50mg IM ($50)
  - B12 injection ($25)
  - Glutathione push ($50)
  - Beauty Boost OR Belly Buster add-on ($50)
  - Branded welcome kit ($35)
- Lifetime lock saves $240/yr forever vs $99 standard
- **Total Year 1 value: ~$629 for $79 today**

### Offer 2: Founding Elite Wellness
Display as **three clean stacked cards or a simple 3-column row** — one card per cohort. Each card shows: cohort number, price/mo, seat count. That's it. Don't overcomplicate it with a gradient fill bar or seat-by-seat dot visualization — just three clean cards side by side.

- **Cohort 1** (first 25): $299/mo locked for life
- **Cohort 2** (next 25): $309/mo locked for life
- **Cohort 3** (next 50): $319/mo locked for life

Below the three cards, show the shared Elite details once:
- Down payment = first month, rolls into MTM
- Standard perks: monthly credit, 50mg NAD+ monthly, Deep Detox IV monthly, Compression + Red Light, 15% off, VIP scheduling
- **Visit One Welcome Bundle (~$815 value)**:
  - Mega Myers IV ($179)
  - Inner Immune OR Repair and Rebuild IV — member's choice ($179)
  - Founder's Choice ($250): NAD IV 250mg OR Foundation Blood Panel + practitioner consult
  - Glutathione push ($50)
  - B12 injection ($25)
  - Premium add-on choice ($50–$72)
  - Premium branded welcome kit ($75)
- Lifetime lock saves $672–$912/yr forever vs $375 standard
- **Total Year 1 value: ~$1,500–$1,725 for $299 today**

### Campaign Arc — horizontal 4-step timeline
- **Phase 1 — Launch (Weeks 1–6)**: Both offers live, Elite Cohort 1 selling, Vital selling, Meta ads + community events, scarcity on Cohort 1 seats
- **Phase 2 — Mid-flight (Weeks 7–12)**: Cohort 1 fills → moves to Cohort 2 ($309), continued Meta + community events, cohort scarcity messaging
- **Phase 3 — T-30 Final Push (Weeks 13–16)**: Cohort 3 ($319) live, "doors open in 30 days, lifetime lock closes at open," aggressive retargeting
- **Open Day (Week 17)**: Founding offer closes, standard pricing only ($99 / $375) forward

### Key Mechanics Callout
- Lifetime rate lock is the **load-bearing promise** across both offers
- Cohort prices escalate as seats fill — urgency without manufactured deadlines
- Founder's Choice (NAD IV vs Foundation Panel) self-segments leads for ad optimization
- Down payment = first month rolling into MTM
- Visit One bundle front-loaded for maximum wow factor
